Chang Fang-Yu (Iris)

Data Scientist · AI Systems · Business-Grounded Analytics

SMU MITB graduate (Data Science & Analytics Track, Scholarship Recipient) with 4+ years of hands-on technical recruiting experience, serving clients ranging from Taiwan's leading semiconductor firms to global tech and fintech multinationals.

After years in tech recruiting, advising both candidates and hiring teams through sales, business development, and stakeholder management, two things pushed me toward data science. I watched AI start to reshape every industry I worked with, and I wanted to be inside that shift, not just placing the people building it. And I realised that my advice, however well-informed, was built on experience and instinct alone. I wanted a more rigorous, evidence-based way to back it up.

That's what brought me back to school, and in every academic project, I pushed to frame HR and recruiting, the business context I knew best, as the problem to solve: designing the analysis around a real decision someone needed to make, not the other way around. That same instinct, start from the business problem, then build the method, is what I bring to any domain, not just the one I came from.

I'm open to Data Scientist, Data Analyst, and AI/Analytics roles. My goal is to go deep in a domain, the way I once went deep in tech recruiting, and become the person who can translate its real business problems into data science solutions, not the other way around.
